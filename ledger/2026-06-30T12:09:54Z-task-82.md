# Task #82: Remove framer-motion layout prop from draggable TaskCard

The `layout` prop was conditionally disabled on the `motion.div` in TaskCard when rendered inside a Draggable wrapper, eliminating layout animation conflicts during drag gestures. Dragging now feels smooth with no lag, snap-back, or jitter, and hovering during drag no longer causes visual jank. Non-draggable cards retain their layout animations where desired.
