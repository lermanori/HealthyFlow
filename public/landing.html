How it works

Dump it. It gets planned.
Then get it done.

No rigid forms to fill. HealthyFlow meets you where your brain is.

STEP 01

Capture

Tasks, ideas, meals, habits, calendar plans — typed or spoken, in plain language. Whatever's on your mind goes in as-is.

STEP 02

Organize

AI structures everything into today's plan — tasks get times, habits recur, meals get macros. Unfinished items roll forward on their own.

STEP 03

Execute

Work through one clear timeline, track progress by life area, and adjust as the day changes — with your streaks intact.

Today's Timeline

Your whole day on one timeline

Tasks, habits, and meals live on a single schedule — not scattered across apps. Drag anything into a time slot, check things off, and watch progress fill in by life area.

  • Drag & drop scheduling with an Anytime backlog
  • Live progress by category — health, work, fitness, and more
  • Incomplete items roll forward automatically
AI Task Assistant

Speak it. It's planned.

Type or dictate what's ahead of you, and the AI analyzer turns it into structured, scheduled items — with the right category, time, and duration. Then Ask AI anything about your own schedule.

  • Natural language in, structured plan out
  • Voice input for hands-free capture
  • Ask AI questions about your tasks and day
Week View

Zoom out without losing the thread

See the whole week at a glance — what's scheduled, what's done, and how consistent your habits have been. Streaks and weekly progress keep the bigger picture honest.

  • Weekly plan with per-day completion
  • Habit consistency grid and streaks
  • Up-next card so you always know what's next
Calories & Weight

Health tracking in the same flow

Log meals in plain language and let Meal AI break them into calories and macros. Weight trends chart automatically alongside your food log — no separate app needed.

  • Meal AI parses food into calories, protein, carbs & fat
  • Weight log with trend chart
  • Daily entries organized by time
Workout Tracker

Log workouts in seconds

Sets, reps, weight, time, distance — logged fast with quick-insert from your exercise history. Your training lives next to your plan, not in another silo.

  • Quick insert from recent and most-used exercises
  • Strength and cardio in one log
  • Session history by day

A note from the founder

HealthyFlow started as the system I built for myself. I had tasks in one app, habits in another, calories in a third, workouts in a fourth — and a calendar that knew nothing about any of them. Every day began with app-switching instead of doing.

So I built one place that takes whatever's in my head, in plain language, and turns it into a day I can actually follow. It changed how I work and how I take care of myself — and now I'd love it to do the same for you.
